To place an order, browse our collection and select the shoes you want. Once you have made your selection, click the "Order via WhatsApp" button. This will redirect you to WhatsApp where you can send us a message with your desired items. We will guide you through the rest of the ordering process.

We accept payments through bank transfer & UPI payments. Payment details will be shared with you by our customer service representative during the WhatsApp checkout process.

Delivery times vary based on your location. Typically, it takes 5-6 business days for orders in the city areas and 7-8 business days for rural areas. Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number to monitor your package.

We apologize for any inconvenience. If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp. Provide us with your order details and a photo of the issue. We will arrange for a replacement or a refund as quickly as possible.

For any inquiries or assistance, you can contact our customer service team via WhatsApp. Click the "Contact Us" button on our website to start a chat with a representative who will be happy to help you.
