| Shipping Policy

At WhiteWalk, we strive to ensure your shopping experience is seamless and satisfactory. Our shipping policy outlines the process and expectations for delivery of your purchases.


1. Order Processing:
  • All products are carefully packed and shipped within 24 to 48 hours of placing your order. This ensures that your items are handled with the utmost care and dispatched promptly.
2. Tracking Your Order:
  • Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an order tracking ID. We will provide you with this tracking ID and assist you in tracking your order until it reaches your doorstep.
3. Delivery Timeframes:
  • Normal Delivery:
    • For urban areas, the typical delivery time is 5 to 6 days.
    • For rural areas, delivery usually takes 6 to 8 days.
  • Express Delivery:
    • If you opt for express delivery, your order will reach you within a maximum of 3 to 4 days.
    • Please note that express delivery incurs a charge of 300 rupees.
4. Delivery Charges:
  • Normal Delivery:
    • We are pleased to offer free shipping for normal delivery. There are no additional charges for this service.
  • Express Delivery:
    • A fee of 300 rupees applies for express delivery, ensuring your order arrives within 3 days.
5. Customer Support:
  • Our customer support team is always ready to help. If you have any questions or concerns about your shipment, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to assist you at every step of the way.

We value your trust and are committed to delivering your purchases efficiently and safely. Thank you for choosing WhiteWalk.

If you have any additional questions or require further assistance, please reach out to our customer service team at contact.whitewalk@gmail.com or +917592984622.
